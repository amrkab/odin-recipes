Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish that combines simplicity and flavor in a harmonious way. To prepare a delicious spaghetti recipe, start by boiling a pot of salted water and cooking the spaghetti until it's al dente. In a separate pan, heat some olive oil and sauté minced garlic until fragrant. Add crushed red pepper flakes for a hint of heat, followed by canned crushed tomatoes, and let it simmer to create a flavorful sauce. Season with salt, pepper, and fresh basil leaves. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain it and combine it with the tomato sauce, allowing the pasta to soak up the flavors. Serve the spaghetti with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves for a delightful and satisfying meal. This classic dish offers a perfect balance of simple, fresh ingredients and comforting taste.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Minced Garlic Cloves
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil the Spaghetti: Fill a large pot with water, add a pinch of salt, and bring it to a boil. Once boiling, add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until it's al dente. Drain the cooked spaghetti and set it aside.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a separate pan, heat a few tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until it becomes fragrant and lightly golden. If you want a bit of heat, add crushed red pepper flakes to taste.
  3. Simmer the Tomato Sauce: Add the canned crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce to the pan, and season with salt and black pepper. Stir well, and then add fresh basil leaves to infuse flavor. Let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Combine the Pasta and Sauce: Once the sauce has developed a rich flavor and the spaghetti is cooked, combine the cooked spaghetti with the tomato sauce. Toss them together to ensure the pasta is coated evenly with the sauce.
  5. Serve: Plate the spaghetti on individual dishes and garnish with more fresh basil leaves and a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Optional Customization: Feel free to customize your spaghetti with additional ingredients like sautéed onions, ground meat (beef, pork, or turkey), or vegetables such as mushrooms, bell peppers, or spinach, based on your preferences.
  7. Enjoy: Serve your delicious spaghetti immediately, and enjoy the wonderful combination of flavors and textures.
